FAQ

Common questions from HVAC owners

What about commercial HVAC customers who pay by check?

Servaia supports cash, check, and invoice customers alongside card customers. You choose how each customer pays when you add them — card customers charge automatically, everyone else you handle manually.

Does this work for maintenance contracts?

Yes — that's the best use case. Once a customer saves their card, every maintenance visit charges automatically when your tech marks it done. No action needed on your end or theirs.

What does it cost?

$49/month flat after a 30-day free trial. Plus standard card processing of 3.5% + $0.30 per transaction — which you can pass to the customer as a surcharge if you choose.

Can my techs use it from the job site?

That's exactly how it works. Your tech opens the app on their phone, marks the job done, and the payment fires. Simple enough for any tech to use on day one.
