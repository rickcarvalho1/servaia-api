How it works

Three steps to same-day payment

Set it up once per customer. Works automatically on every job after that.

1

Customer Saves Their Card

Before the first job, you send a secure link. They save their card once. That's the last time payment is a conversation — everything after is automatic.

2

Electrician Marks the Job Done

When the work is finished, your electrician marks the job complete from their phone. The charge fires instantly — right from the job site.

You Get Paid Same Day

The payment hits your account the same day. The customer gets a receipt automatically. No office admin, no follow-up calls, no waiting on a check.

FAQ

Common questions from electrical contractors

What about jobs with time and materials billing?

You set the final amount when you mark the job done — so T&M jobs work fine. Your electrician enters the final total on-site and the charge fires for that exact amount.

What about commercial customers who pay by check or NET30?

Servaia supports cash, check, and invoice customers alongside card customers. Card customers charge automatically — everyone else you handle on your normal terms.

What does it cost?

$49/month flat after a 30-day free trial. Plus standard card processing of 3.5% + $0.30 per transaction — which you can pass to the customer as a surcharge if you choose.

Is customer card data secure?

Yes — Servaia uses Stripe to store and process all card data. Card numbers are never stored on our servers. Stripe is PCI Level 1 certified, the highest level of payment security.
